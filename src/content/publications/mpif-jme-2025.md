title: "Physical Information Fusion of Multi-modal Data for Online Fault Monitoring of Selective Laser Melting Integrated Manufacturing"
abstract: "Online fault monitoring of selective laser melting is limited by offline detection and weak physics-aware multi-modal fusion. MPIF-Net combines dual-attention convolution and Mamba over fused acoustic-emission images, a BiLSTM over raw signals, and a PINN-generated thermal modality to capture both physical and data-driven cues. The unified channel-concatenation fusion yields a 1.48% F1 improvement over conventional multi-modal baselines."
