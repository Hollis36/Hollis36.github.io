title: "Deep learning enabled magnetic/rare earth hybrid nanorobots for multi-modal bioimaging and temperature sensing with surgical boundary determination"
abstract: "A magnetic/rare-earth hybrid nanorobot platform is proposed for precision biomedicine, combining ferric-oxide substrate with rare-earth nanoparticles to enable NIR-II fluorescence, MRI, and photoacoustic imaging plus dual-targeting tumor delivery under an external magnetic field. Deep learning over NIR-II images simultaneously performs fluorescence boundary determination and temperature sensing (R² = 0.973, RMSE = 1.50 °C across 24.6–41.5 °C)."
