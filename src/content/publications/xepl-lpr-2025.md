title: "Dual-Heterovalent Codoping Enables Bright and Rapid X-Ray Excited Persistent Luminescence for Noise-Free 3D Imaging and Intelligent Detection"
abstract: "Continuous X-ray irradiation generates scattered photons that degrade scintillator imaging; we address this with X-ray-excited persistent luminescence from dual-heterovalent codoped SrFCl phosphors. A trap–Ce–Tb cascade plus Na⁺ co-doping yields a 13.4× Tb³⁺ XEPL enhancement and a 21.4 lp/mm spatial resolution after only 5 s of exposure, enabling delayed, background-free 3D reconstruction and machine-learning-driven intelligent detection."
